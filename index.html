Our Project

UJCivil Club System

Transforming Student Society Management

The UJCivil Club System is an innovative platform designed specifically for the University of Johannesburg's Civils Student Society. Our goal is to streamline club management, making it easier for members to communicate, organize events, and manage finances.

Key Features:
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Event Management System
  • Member Communication Tools
  • Financial Tracking
  • Data Analytics and Reporting

Join us in revolutionizing club management and enhancing the student experience at UJ!
